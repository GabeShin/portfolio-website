# Mobile gaze-tracking model research

## Problem

State-of-the-art gaze tracking research existed in academic papers but didn't hold up on mobile cameras at production-usable accuracy and latency. The team needed the gap closed if the SDK was going to ship.

## What I built

- Researched and reproduced state-of-the-art gaze-tracking models, then translated those findings into product improvements.
- Partnered with the engineering team to build crowdsourced data-collection workflows and improve training-data quality.
- Iterated on model architecture and training to fit mobile constraints.

## Outcomes

- Improved model accuracy by 25%, reaching state-of-the-art performance in mobile gaze tracking.
- Foundational research that fed directly into the commercial SeeSo SDK.

## Notes

- The transition from research to product owner happened mid-2020 when this work fed into SDK delivery — see `seeso-sdk.md`.
