# Enhans

## What Enhans does

Enhans is a South Korea-based AI startup that develops LAM (Large Action Model)-based action-oriented AI agents and a commerce automation operating system (AgentOS, formerly CommerceOS — rename in progress).

## My role

AI Engineer, Apr 2025 – Present. Architecting and shipping browser automation, production-scale matching systems, and agentic-loop tooling. Working directly with enterprise customers to turn domain knowledge into automation logic and proof-of-concept integrations.

## Focus periods inside Enhans

The reference Korean resume splits Enhans work into two ownership periods. Both projects are still in production; the dates below describe Gabe's primary focus.

- **2025.04 – 2025.12 — Identical product matching pipeline (AgentOS dynamic pricing)**. Architected and operated the three-stage matching system. See `projects/agentos-matching.md` and `projects/title-extractor.md`.
- **2025.12 – present — ACT-2 browser agent**. Architected the SDK → Runtime → Brain platform; built deterministic replay/workflow capabilities and the AFK Research Test Harness for agentic-loop development. See `projects/act-2.md` and `projects/afk-harness.md`.

Customer-facing scope includes scoping and prototyping enterprise solutions with product managers across large Korean enterprises in internet, conglomerate-retail, and food-distribution sectors.
